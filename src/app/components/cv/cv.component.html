About Me

“ – Animators are brilliant actors, too! They can act out all kinds of roles! Not just humans, but wind, nature and machines too! It can reach greater expressive heights than live actors ever could!”

"- Eizouken ni wa Te wo Dasu na!. "

I’m Max (21). I have a bachelor’s degree in IT and, in 2021, I have successfully finished the “Digital Art” course in ArtCraft school.

I started drawing when I was 13. While studying at art school I was trying different styles to understand my purpose in this sphere. Only by the age of 17 I realized that digital art is my craft, so I began to improve my skills.

"Animation is my main source of wisdom and inspiration. It’s important to always have a goal and never stop trying to reach it no matter what! I want to animate! "
